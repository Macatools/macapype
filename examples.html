Pipeline examples¶

Macaque:¶

  • Segmentation of PNH test dataset processed with SPM-based full pipeline.

Origin of the dataset: ERC COVOPRIM project.

Acknowledgements to Pascal Belin and Regis Trapeau.


  • Segmentation of PNH test dataset processed with ANTS-based full pipeline.

Origin of the dataset: ERC COVOPRIM project.

Acknowledgements to Pascal Belin and Regis Trapeau.


  • Segmentation of PNH test dataset with reorientation, starting from a sphinx position, processed with ANTS-based full pipeline.

Acknowledgements to Frederic Chavane and Isabelle Racicot.


Marmoset:¶

  • Segmentation of marmoset test dataset, processed using same ANTS-based full pipeline.

The automated cropping did not work, hence manual cropping was used, cropbox has to be specified manually.

Origin of the dataset: ANR Marmobrain project.

Acknowledgements to Frederic Chavane, Thomas Brochier and Julien Sein.


Baboon:¶

  • Segmentation of baboon test dataset, processed using same ANTS-based full pipeline, with single_preparation pipeline for data_preparation.

Origin of the dataset: ERC GestImage project.

Acknowledgements to Adrien Meguerditchian and Yannick Becker.
